- How can Pablo know if his Smart Lock and Smart Doorbell are working properly after following the instructions?

He can also try to send a message to his Smart Lock and Smart Doorbell to check if they receive it. If they do not work properly, he may need to contact the manufacturer or the customer support for assistance.

- Why is it important to use a strong password for the Smart Lock and Smart Doorbell?

Possible answer: It is important to use a strong password for the Smart Lock and Smart Doorbell because a strong password makes it harder for an attacker to guess or crack the password and access the devices. A strong password should be long, complex, and unique, and not based on personal information or common words. A strong password can protect the security and privacy of the Smart Home and the user.

- How can Pablo prevent future intrusions on his Smart Home Devices?

Possible answer: Pablo can prevent future intrusions on his Smart Home Devices by following some best practices of cybersecurity, such as:

- Keeping the devices and the software updated with the latest patches and fixes.
- Changing the default or shared passwords of the devices and the software to something more secure.
- Scanning the devices and the software for malware or viruses regularly.
- Using a firewall or an antivirus program on his phone or computer.
- Avoiding clicking on suspicious links or attachments or downloading unknown files.
- Using a VPN or a secure network when accessing the devices remotely.
- Educating himself about the common types and signs of cyberattacks and how to avoid them.
